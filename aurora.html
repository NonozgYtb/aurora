    Welcome to Aurora Bookmarks

    Basic Info

    Aurora Bookmarks uses your browser bookmarks and displays them in easy to read Boards and Lists. All changes made in the Aurora Bookmarks are synced and backed up with Google. Once you have created your boards and lists you can continue to use your bookmarks as before, with the added benefit of visual categorisation.

    To start simply click on Edit Bookmarks button on the left panel and select tripple dot icon in the top right corner of the Bookmarks window, then select Add New Folder. You can drag or rename the folders in this window to create your desire board/list structure.

    Correct folder structure should look like this:

    folder Other Bookmarks arrow_forward folder My Board arrow_forward folder My List arrow_forward star My Bookmark(s)

    Sturucture

    Boards

    Boards are created from the first level of the Bookmarks arrow_forward Other Bookmarks folder. The board navigation panel will only list folders and not bookmarks. If you have any bookmarks directly in Other Bookmarks folder, move them into your desired list subfolder.

    Lists

    Lists are created from the second level of the folders within the directory above. If you have uncategorised bookmarks within your board folder, the Aurora Bookmarks will create a list called Uncategorised where all your uncategorised bookmarks will be listed. Within a single list you can mix bookmarks as well as folders. Every next level of folders from here will be treated as a subcategory and listed as a heading.

    Reordering

    The default order of your lists is from the left to right. The order of your boards, list & bookmarks reflects the folder order in the Bookmark window. In addition to the default order you have the ability to move an entire list to the next column and leave a blank space in between columns. To make a space between lists just create an empty folder within your board with the name "---".

    Featured Bookmarks

    By default Aurora will create a folder called *Aurora Featured* within your browser bookmarks to which you can drag any bookmark you wish to display on your Dashboard. You can hide Featured Bookmarks within the settings however the folder will not be deleted.
